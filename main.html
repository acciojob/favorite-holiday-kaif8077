Goa


Goa is a beautiful coastal destination in India, famous for its beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.

  1. Baga Beach – known for vibrant nightlife
  2. Calangute Beach – the largest beach in Goa
  3. Fort Aguada – a historic Portuguese fort
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – one of India’s tallest waterfalls
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus – a UNESCO World Heritage site
  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchão
  • Bebinca – a traditional dessert
  • Pork Vindaloo
  • Sol Kadhi
