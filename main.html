Goa


Goa is a popular holiday destination known for its beaches, nightlife, and scenic beauty.

  1. Baga Beach – famous for nightlife
  2. Calangute Beach – the largest beach in Goa
  3. Fort Aguada – a historic fort
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – breathtaking waterfalls
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus – UNESCO heritage site
  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchao
  • Bebinca
  • Pork Vindaloo
  • Sol Kadhi
